Our Services

Custom Machining & Fabrication

Our machining services produce accurate, high-quality metal and non-metal components:

  • CNC machining with tight tolerances
  • Turning and milling of shafts, flanges, and fittings
  • Grinding and honing for surface durability
  • Custom thread cutting and rolling

Valve Refurbishment & Repair Services

We extend valve lifespan and restore full performance through:

  • Inspection and diagnostics
  • Cleaning and corrosion removal
  • Component rebuilding and machining
  • Protective sealing and coating
  • Pressure testing and calibration

On-site Installation & Maintenance Support

Our on-site support team provides installation, commissioning, and maintenance services to ensure your equipment operates at peak performance:

  • Installation of new valve systems
  • Commissioning and system testing
  • Regular maintenance and inspection schedules
  • Emergency repair services
